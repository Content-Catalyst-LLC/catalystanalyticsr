# Catalyst Analytics R v0.6.0

## Climate, Carbon, and Natural-Capital Accounting

This release creates the first governed sustainability-accounting layer in Catalyst Analytics R.

### Emissions inventory

`catalyst_emissions_inventory` normalizes gross emissions, removals, and net emissions while retaining the dataset fingerprint, source, unit, accounting basis, GWP statement, entity and time keys, gas categories, source categories, and optional Kaya fields.

### Carbon budget

`catalyst_carbon_pathway` reports cumulative gross emissions, cumulative removals, cumulative net emissions, remaining budget, budget share, overshoot and recovery timing, target-year values, post-target positive emissions, lock-in share, and stranded-pathway signals.

### Kaya decomposition

The package calculates population, affluence, energy intensity, and carbon intensity and uses additive log-mean Divisia decomposition to reconcile changes in gross emissions.

### Natural capital

`catalyst_natural_capital_account` reconciles opening stock, regeneration, restoration, additions, extraction, degradation, damages, expected closing stock, observed closing stock, and reconciliation error.

### Boundary assessment

Versioned boundary definitions support at-or-below, at-or-above, and inside-range tests with warning margins and unit checks. These are review signals, not compliance determinations.

### Public companion

WordPress demo v1.5.0 performs deterministic browser calculations for an educational pathway and exports the mapped climate contract. It does not execute R or validate a real inventory, carbon budget, GWP basis, or natural-capital valuation.
