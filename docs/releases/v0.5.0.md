# Catalyst Analytics R v0.5.0

## Data Intake and Indicator Registry

This release creates the governed data and indicator layer required for later calibration, validation, climate accounting, and inclusive-wealth analysis.

### Dataset contract

`catalyst_dataset` preserves records together with source, license, citation, retrieval metadata, units, geographic and sector scope, currency and price year, required fields, missing-value policy, quality flags, transformation history, and a stable fingerprint.

### Indicator registry

`catalyst_indicator` definitions preserve a semantic version, formula, required fields, unit, direction, aggregation method, methodology source, targets, and executable calculation callback. Results preserve a complete trace to the dataset fingerprint and source record.

### Public companion

WordPress demo v1.4.0 accepts tidy CSV input, reports quality issues, calculates a selected indicator, displays its definition, and exports a governed browser record. It remains an educational companion rather than a source-verification or compliance system.
