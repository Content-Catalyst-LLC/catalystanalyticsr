# Catalyst Analytics R v0.7.0

## Inclusive Wealth, Human Development, and Distribution

This release adds a governed multidimensional development layer above the existing data, scenario, uncertainty, and climate-accounting foundations.

### Capital and wealth accounting

- Produced-, human-, and natural-capital stock-and-flow accounts
- Explicit investment, depreciation, depletion, damages, revaluation, and reconciliation
- Declared shadow prices and valued stock changes
- Inclusive wealth, capital composition, and per-capita wealth

### Development and savings

- Adjusted Net Savings decomposition
- Education and health investment additions
- Produced-capital depreciation, resource depletion, pollution damages, and climate damages
- Human-development life, education, income, and combined indices

### Distribution and generations

- Weighted mean, median, quantiles, Gini, Palma, top-10, and bottom-40 shares
- Social-floor exposure
- Group summaries
- Per-capita wealth trajectories across declared generation lengths
- Optional social discounting and targets

### Composite governance

- Explicit component definitions, directions, weights, and normalization bounds
- Missing-value policy
- Component contribution trace
- Weight-sensitivity diagnostics

- R package and repository: `0.7.0`

- Browser export contract: `1.6.0`
