# Inclusive Wealth, Human Development, and Distribution

## Accounting identity

Each capital account uses:

```text
closing stock = opening stock + investment + revaluation
                - depreciation - depletion - damages
```

The difference between an observed closing stock and the accounting identity remains visible as a reconciliation error.

## Inclusive wealth

Inclusive wealth is the sum of valued produced, human, and natural capital:

```text
inclusive wealth = produced stock * produced shadow price
                 + human stock * human shadow price
                 + natural stock * natural shadow price
```

Per-capita wealth divides the valued total by a documented positive population. Shadow prices are declared analytical weights and require review.

## Adjusted Net Savings

```text
ANS = gross savings
    + education investment
    + health investment
    + other adjustments
    - produced-capital depreciation
    - natural-resource depletion
    - pollution damages
    - climate damages
```

## Human development

Life expectancy, schooling, and logarithmic income indices are bounded between zero and one. The combined index is their geometric mean. Goalposts remain explicit and reviewable.

## Distribution

The distribution object preserves values, weights, groups, weighted summaries, quantiles, Gini, Palma, top-10 and bottom-40 shares, and the share below a declared social floor.

## Composite scores

Composite scores expose components, directions, bounds, normalized weights, missing-data policy, normalized values, weighted contributions, and score movement under weight perturbations. They are review aids, not autonomous rankings.
