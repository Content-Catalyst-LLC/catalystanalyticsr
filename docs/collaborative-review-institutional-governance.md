# Collaborative Review and Institutional Governance

Catalyst Analytics R v1.6.0 adds a governed institutional review layer above reproducible projects and workspaces.

## Core records

- role and actor definitions for analysts, reviewers, approvers, publishers, and administrators
- review assignments and structured comments
- change requests and resolutions
- scoped approval decisions
- signed analytical releases linked to project and artifact fingerprints
- institutional templates and restricted-access policies
- complete audit, retention, archival, and disposal-eligibility records

## Decision boundary

The package records institutional decisions but does not authenticate identities, provide legal electronic signatures, authorize publication automatically, or replace institutional policy. A host platform must supply identity, access enforcement, durable storage, notification, and signature services where required.
